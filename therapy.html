Therapy

I offer some world class therapies. Below you see a detailed approach of what you would expect in one of our classes. so have a look and join us on your mission to keep wonderful. I understand not all therapies will match your needs hence we have widened our selection.

YOGA

Yoga originated in ancient India and is a group of physical, mental and spiritual practices or disciplines which have multiple benefits for the body. We offer a tranquil and open space in order to find your zen

45 minute sessions for only £45

PILATES

Pilates aims to strengthen the body in an even way with particular emphasis on core strength. It improves posture, muscle tone, balance and joint mobility. It relieves the body of stress and tension, all takes place on our beach boardwalk setting.

30 minute sessions for only £30

REIKI

Reiki is a form of energy healing. It uses tecniques called palm healing to transfer a universal energy through the palms of the practitioner to the patient in order to encourage emotional, physical and spiritual healing.

30 minute sessions for only £25

HYPNOTHERAPY

Hypnotherapy uses guided relaxation, intense concentration and focused attention to achieve a heightened state of awareness similar to a trance. Hynotherapy is used to adress undesirable behaviours.

45 minute sessions for only £25

MEDITATION

Meditation aims to get the body into a mentally clear and emtionally stable and tranquil state, to train attention and awareness through techniques such as mindfulness and concentration. A method that is proven across all age groups.

45 minute sessions for only £50

HOT STONE MASSAGE

Hot stone massage works through the placement of smooth water heated stones placed at key points on the body. It melts away tension in the body, eases muscle stiffness, increases circulation and speeds up metabolism.

60 minute sessions for only £50
